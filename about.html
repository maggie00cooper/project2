ABOUT US

Brown Bag Deli is a deli style sandwich shop
located in the heart of downtown Louisville.
We take pride in our sandwich's and
we hope you find them as delicious as we do.

OUR MISSION

Our mission at Brown Bag Deli
is to provide fresh, hot, & ready to go sandwiches.
We are a family owned mom & pop shop
and we take pride in all we do.
We plan to create a memorable and
fun experience for anyone who comes to our deli.
We hope our sandwich and other food options are able
to satisfy anyone who walks in our doors needs.
Thank your for stopping to read about us
and we hope to see you soon at Brown Bag Deli.
